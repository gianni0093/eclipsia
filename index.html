ECLIPSIA — A Dual-Realm Fantasy Universe

Created by Gianni Ezio Barbato

Overview

ECLIPSIA is a complete fantasy universe designed for AAA interactive media. Built around the dual realms of Aurelia (Revealed Light) and Umbra (Contained Shadow), the world combines emotional progression, deep cosmology, and scalable worldbuilding for narrative-driven or action RPG experiences.

Core Features

• Dual-realm world structure

• Emotional progression system (CURVA B)

• Scalable biomes, regions, dungeons, and bosses

• Strong thematic identity and narrative foundations

• High potential for transmedia adaptation

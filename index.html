ECLIPSIA

A Dual-Realm Fantasy Universe
Created by Gianni Ezio Barbato

Overview

ECLIPSIA is a fully realized fantasy universe designed for AAA interactive media. Built around the dual realms of Aurelia (Revealed Light) and Umbra (Contained Shadow), the world blends mythological balance, emotional resonance, and large-scale narrative potential.

The world is structured for action RPG, open-world, and narrative-driven productions, offering a mature emotional identity and complete production-ready foundations.

Core Features

• Dual-Realm world structure (Aurelia & Umbra)

• Emotional progression system (CURVA B)

• Scalable regions, biomes, dungeons, and cities

• Boss and multi-phase encounter frameworks

• Rich narrative and mythological architecture

• High transmedia potential (game, animation, media)
